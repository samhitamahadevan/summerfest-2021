SUMMER FEST 2021

Summerfest, the annual cultural fest of the College Of Engineering, Goa, offers a wide variety of technical and non-technical events, designed to keep young minds enthralled! This year, Summerfest is proud to present 10+ technical and non-technical events, organized by the student committee!In addition to the events, this year marks the first year of a women exclusive coding hackathon 'Code For Queens', in an attempt to encourage women in STEM to pursue their passion for coding. This year, Summerfest will take place on the 4th and 5th of June, looking forward to seeing you there!
